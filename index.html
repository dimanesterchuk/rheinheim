About company

RHEINHEIM S.A. is an international supplier of raw materials and high-performance protective coatings under Welesgard brand for a wide range of industrial applications to its international clients. The company was established in 2008 in Switzerland, since that import and export activities are supported with logistical support and technical assistance from early project planning, application procedures and inspections.
